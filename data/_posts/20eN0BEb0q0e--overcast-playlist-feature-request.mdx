Feature requests for Overcast Podcast App:

1. Create a playlist of different podcasts. 
2. Set a minimum of time to play in each (e.g. 10 minutes)
3. Enter a Round Robin loop playing through the podcasts starting from oldest to newest. 
4. At least one episode from each podcast would be played. 
5. If that episode didn't push the total time for the current run over the minimum time, play the next episode. 
6. Repeat until the total time spent in a shot goes over the minimum time, then move to the next show on the list.
