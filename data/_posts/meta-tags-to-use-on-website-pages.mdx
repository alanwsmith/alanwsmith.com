Here's the meta tags from the google home page:


```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/WebPagee" lang="en">
    <head>
        <meta charset="UTF-8">
        <meta content="dark" name="color-scheme">
        <meta content="origin" name="referrer">
        <meta content="/images/branding/googleg/1x/googleg_standard_color_128dp.png" itemprop="image">
        <link href="/manifest?pwa=webhp" crossorigin="use-credentials" rel="manifest">
        <title>Google</title>
        <!-- a bunch of style sheets and scripts -->
```


Here's the meta tags from the head of a google search:

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/SearchResultsPage" lang="en">
```
