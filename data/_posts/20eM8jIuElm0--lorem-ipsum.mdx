For those designers of you out there, if you are working on a
layout and you need some faux text, the site
[lipsum.com](http://www.lipsum.com/) will provide you with the as
much of the good ol' "Lorem Ipsum" standby as you want. I used to
use snippets from
[Augustine's Confessions](http://ccat.sas.upenn.edu/jod/latinconf/latinconf.html)
until I found that site. For those of you lost at this point,
here's part of an explanation from the lipsum.com site:
> It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by
> the readable content of a page when looking at its layout. The
> point of using Lorem Ipsum is that it has a more-or-less normal
> distribution of letters, as opposed to using 'Content here, content
> here', making it look like readable English.
