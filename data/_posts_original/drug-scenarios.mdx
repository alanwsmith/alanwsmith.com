Looking around freevibe.com (the federal government's anti-drug web
site) you can see
[what will happen](http://www.freevibe.com/Drug_Facts/scenarios.asp)
to you if you smoke pot. Be sure to watch all three outcomes for
each scenario so you will know all the possible outcomes. Another
good read is
"[Why People Take Drugs](http://www.freevibe.com/Drug_Facts/why_drugs.asp)".
