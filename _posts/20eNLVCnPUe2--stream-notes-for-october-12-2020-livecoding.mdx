### [Time: 00:34:20] - Automatically Moving Screenshots Into My Directory Structure

Lesson: Don't put quotes around paths in launchd scripts. It makes them explode. 

Here's what worked:

```xml{numberLines: true}
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<!DOCTYPE plist PUBLIC "-//Apple//DTD PLIST 1.0//EN" "http://www.apple.com/DTDs/PropertyList-1.0.dtd">
<plist version="1.0">
<dict>

    <key>Label</key>
    <string>com.alanwsmith.rename_screenshots.plist</string>
    
    <key>ProgramArguments</key>
    <array>
        <string>/usr/local/bin/python3</string>
        <string>/Users/alans/toolkit/rename_screenshots/rename_screenshots.py</string>
    </array>

    <key>WatchPaths</key>
    <array>
        <string>/Users/alans/Screenshots/_incoming</string>
    </array>

</dict>
</plist>
```
