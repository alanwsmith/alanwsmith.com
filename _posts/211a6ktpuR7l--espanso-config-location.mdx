### Overview:

Make an individual file for each expansion under:

    ~/Library/Preferences/espanso/user

Put support scripts in:

    ~/Library/Preferences/espanso/user


The overall config is done with:

    ~/Library/Preferences/espanso/default.yml


---

All those are sym linked to corresponding locations under

    ~/workshop/dotfiles.alanwsmith.com/dotfiles/espanso
