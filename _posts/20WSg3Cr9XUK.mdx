I flew up to ATL on Tuesday of this week which was Sept. 11. It was
a little odd to fly on that day, but by and large I didn't sense
any more tension on the flight than normal. Even with a completely
full plane. Only thing I noticed was a few extra security guards
outside the jax airport.
