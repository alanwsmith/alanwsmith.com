Reloading ZSH
=============

If changes are made the necessitate zsh restarting to take effect (e.g. updating ~/.zshrc), the way to do it is with:

	exec zsh


Details [https://www.shell-tips.com/linux/how-to-reload-shell/](here)


---

I had a note saying this doesn't always work, but I'm not sure what the problem was. So, keep an eye out.
