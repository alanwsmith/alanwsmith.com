```python
range(5, -1, -1)
```

outputs:

```
[5,4,3,2,1,0]
```


This uses `range(start, stop, step)` with the step being negative to walk backwards instead of forwards.




---

Other options are:

reversed(range(10))
and
list(reversed(range(10)))
