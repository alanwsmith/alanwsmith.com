This may seem straightforward, but it always confuses me. So, under Advanced Audio Properties:


- If the video is ahead of the audio, then put in a negative sync offset (e.g. -150ms)

- If the audio is in front of the video, then put in a positive sync offset (e.g. 150ms)

June 20, 2021 - After moving the camera to 1920x1080 (FHD), did a series of tests and dialed in at +48ms when going through the Adobe Audition and SnapCam for Audio and Video
